## A. STARTUP & BUSINESS REGISTRATION (10 Services)

### 1. Proprietorship Registration
- **Description**: Register a sole proprietorship business
- **Key Features**: Simple registration, minimal compliance
- **Documents**: PAN, Aadhaar, Address proof, Bank account
- **Processing Time**: 7-15 days
- **Use Case**: Individual entrepreneurs, small businesses

### 2. Partnership Firm Registration
- **Description**: Register a partnership business
- **Key Features**: Partnership deed, multiple partners
- **Documents**: Partnership deed, PAN of partners, Address proof
- **Processing Time**: 10-20 days
- **Use Case**: Small businesses with 2+ partners

### 3. One Person Company (OPC) Registration
- **Description**: Register a company with single director
- **Key Features**: Limited liability, single member
- **Documents**: DIN, DSC, MOA, AOA, Address proof
- **Processing Time**: 15-25 days
- **Use Case**: Solo entrepreneurs wanting company structure

### 4. Limited Liability Partnership (LLP) Registration
- **Description**: Register an LLP with limited liability
- **Key Features**: Limited liability, flexible structure
- **Documents**: LLP agreement, DIN, DSC, Address proof
- **Processing Time**: 15-30 days
- **Use Case**: Professional services, startups

### 5. Private Limited Company Registration
- **Description**: Register a private limited company
- **Key Features**: Limited liability, separate legal entity
- **Documents**: DIN, DSC, MOA, AOA, Address proof, Bank statements
- **Processing Time**: 15-30 days
- **Pricing**: ₹6,899 + GST (inclusive of government fees)
- **Use Case**: Most common business structure, scalable

### 6. Section 8 Company Registration
- **Description**: Register a non-profit company
- **Key Features**: No profit distribution, charitable purpose
- **Documents**: MOA, AOA, License from Central Government
- **Processing Time**: 30-45 days
- **Use Case**: NGOs, charitable organizations

### 7. Trust Registration
- **Description**: Register a trust for charitable purposes
- **Key Features**: Trust deed, trustees, charitable objectives
- **Documents**: Trust deed, Identity proof of trustees
- **Processing Time**: 20-30 days
- **Use Case**: Charitable trusts, religious organizations

### 8. Public Limited Company Registration
- **Description**: Register a public limited company
- **Key Features**: Can raise public funds, listed companies
- **Documents**: MOA, AOA, DIN, DSC, Prospectus
- **Processing Time**: 30-45 days
- **Use Case**: Large businesses, public offerings

### 9. Producer Company Registration
- **Description**: Register a producer company (agriculture)
- **Key Features**: For agricultural producers, cooperative structure
- **Documents**: MOA, AOA, Producer details
- **Processing Time**: 20-30 days
- **Use Case**: Agricultural cooperatives, farmer groups

### 10. Indian Subsidiary Registration
- **Description**: Register a subsidiary of foreign company
- **Key Features**: Foreign investment, compliance with FEMA
- **Documents**: Foreign company documents, Indian director details
- **Processing Time**: 30-45 days
- **Use Case**: Foreign companies entering India
