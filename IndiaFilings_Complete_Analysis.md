# IndiaFilings Website - Complete Analysis & Research Report

## Executive Summary

IndiaFilings is India's largest AI-powered corporate services and compliance platform, established in 2014. The platform serves over 1 lakh (100,000+) businesses with a team of 600+ employees. It provides end-to-end business registration, compliance, and tax filing services through an AI-powered platform with intelligent automation.

**Website URL:** https://www.indiafilings.com

## 1. BUSINESS MODEL & VALUE PROPOSITION

### Core Business Model
- **B2B2C Platform**: Acts as an intermediary between businesses/entrepreneurs and government departments
- **Service-Based Revenue**: Charges fees for each service provided
- **AI-Powered Automation**: Uses AI to automate compliance workflows, form filling, and document processing
- **Expert Support**: Provides professional CA/CS support alongside automated services

### Key Value Propositions
1. **Simplified Compliance**: Makes complex regulatory processes simple
