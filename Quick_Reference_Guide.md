# Quick Reference Guide - IndiaFilings Analysis

## 🎯 Core Business Model
- **Type**: B2B2C Platform (Business to Business to Consumer)
- **Revenue**: Service fees + Government fees + GST
- **Differentiator**: AI-powered automation + Expert CA/CS support
- **Scale**: 100,000+ businesses, 600+ employees, 10+ years
